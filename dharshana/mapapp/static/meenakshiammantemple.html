The Meenakshi Amman Temple in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, is a magnificent Dravidian architectural marvel dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi (Parvati) and Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva), famous for its towering, sculpture-laden gopurams (gateway towers), intricate carvings, thousand-pillar hall, and a sacred temple tank, forming a sprawling complex that's a significant Hindu pilgrimage site and cultural icon
